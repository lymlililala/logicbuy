The PitfallFree Expert Team is a group of independent researchers, engineers, and consumer advocates dedicated to one mission: helping you make smarter purchases based on objective specs — not brand marketing.

We analyze product specifications, industry standards, and real-world performance data so you know exactly what to look for (and what to avoid) before spending your money.

**Our principles:**

- 🚫 No brand partnerships or sponsored recommendations
- 📊 Every recommendation is backed by measurable specifications
- ✅ We test checklists against real purchase scenarios
- 🌍 Built for global consumers, written in plain English
